VOLUNTEER EXPERIENCE

Volunteering is an incredibly rewarding and fulfilling experience that allows individuals to make a positive impact on their communities and the lives of others. As a volunteer, I have had the opportunity to contribute my time and skills to various organizations and causes, and it has been an enriching journey.

One of my most significant volunteer experiences was working with a local non-profit organization that focused on providing education and support to underprivileged children. In this role, I helped facilitate after-school programs, tutoring sessions, and educational workshops. It was incredibly inspiring to witness the enthusiasm and eagerness of these children to learn and succeed despite the challenges they faced. Being able to contribute to their educational growth and seeing their progress was immensely gratifying.

Through my volunteer work, I had the opportunity to contribute to CIAS's efforts to promote astronomical education. I was able to create graphics that helped to promote events and initiatives, raise awareness about astronomical research, and engage the public in the organization's mission. Additionally, I gained valuable experience in design software such as Adobe Photoshop and in collaborating with a team to bring creative projects to fruition.

I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked with such a dedicated and passionate organization and to have been a part of promoting astronomical research and education.

LECTUTER / INSTRUCTOR
Central Initiative for Astronomical Studies - (Obervation / Rocketry)

Observation astronomy and rocketry are fascinating fields that explore the mysteries of the universe and our ability to reach beyond our planet. Observation astronomy involves studying celestial objects and phenomena using telescopes and other instruments, unraveling the secrets of distant galaxies, stars, and planets. It allows us to understand the origins of the universe and our place within it. Rocketry, on the other hand, focuses on the design, construction, and launch of rockets into space. It enables us to explore space, conduct scientific experiments, and even envision future space travel and colonization. Both observation astronomy and rocketry open doors to endless possibilities, expanding our knowledge and igniting our curiosity about the vastness of the cosmos.

SASNAKA SANSADA FOUNDATION

The Foundation Stone of Sasnaka Sansada was laid in 1997 by a group of young individuals who were gathered to the Sri Sudassanarama Temple in Battaramulla, who subsequently got involved with a number of socio-religious activities with the involvement of the villages and other well wishers.The initial deliberations of Sasnaka Sansada aimed to inculcate Buddhist spiritual essence among the younger generation who were in the membership and get their involvement in the social and community acts.
After the initiation, Sasnaka Sansada expanded its activities to the other region and parts of the island.First of such activity was at a very rural school in Ratnapura District where the foundation provided educational aids to school children and spent the entire day with them educating and inspiring those children with social events.Many books, stationary items, school items, clothes, shoes and other collected items were distributed among the students.
